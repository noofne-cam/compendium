
---
- **A.** A **group** is a set $G$ augmented with a binary operator $\cdot$ (or $+$), which satisfy:
	- i. the condition of *closure* of $\cdot$ under $G$;
	- ii. the *associativity* of $\cdot$ under $G$;
	- iii. the existence of an *identity* $1$ wrt $\cdot$ under $G$;
	- iv. the condition of *inversion* of $\cdot$ under $G$.
- Additionally, if $\cdot$ is *commutative* under $G$ the group is called **abelian**.
